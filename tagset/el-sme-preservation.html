Element: sme:preservation

This records a commitment from a publisher or archive to carry the supplemental material forward. Used to help determine the likelihood that the material will be accessible in the future.

Content Model

( sme:preservation-level, sme:preservation-rationale?, sme:preservation-authority+ )

May Contain:

  • <sme:preservation-authority>
  • <sme:preservation-level>
  • <sme:preservation-rationale>

May be contained in:

  • <fig>
  • <sme:item>
  • <supplementary-material>
