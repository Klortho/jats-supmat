Documentation for NISO-supmat-extension-rec0.dtd

Element: person-group

Person Group for a Cited Publication
Container element for one or more contributors in a bibliographic reference.

Attributes

  • @id
  • @person-group-type
  • @specific-use
  • @xml:lang

Content Model

( #PCDATA | anonymous | collab | collab-alternatives | name | name-alternatives | string-name | aff | aff-alternatives | etal | role )*

May Contain:

  • PCDATA
  • <aff>
  • <aff-alternatives>
  • <anonymous>
  • <collab>
  • <collab-alternatives>
  • <etal>
  • <name>
  • <name-alternatives>
  • <role>
  • <string-name>

May be contained in:

  • <element-citation>
  • <mixed-citation>
  • <nlm-citation>
  • <product>
  • <related-article>
  • <related-object>
