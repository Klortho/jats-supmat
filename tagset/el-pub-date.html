Documentation for NISO-supmat-extension-rec0.dtd

Element: pub-date

Publication Date
Container element for the parts of a single date associated with an article's publication (for example, date preprint was released, date article was published in print).

Attributes

  • @calendar
  • @date-type
  • @iso-8601-date
  • @pub-type
  • @publication-format
  • @xml:lang

Content Model

( ( ( day?, month? ) | season )?, year?, string-date? )

May Contain:

  • <day>
  • <month>
  • <season>
  • <string-date>
  • <year>

May be contained in:

  • <article-meta>
  • <front-stub>
