Element: table-wrap

Table Wrapper
Wrapper element for a complete table, including the tabular material (rows and columns), caption (including title), footnotes, and alternative descriptions of the table for purposes of accessibility.

  • @id
  • @orientation
  • @position
  • @specific-use
  • @xml:lang
