Element: title-group

Title Group
Container element to hold the various titles for a document or a document component.

Content Model

( article-title, subtitle*, trans-title-group*, alt-title*, fn-group? )

May Contain:

  • <alt-title>
  • <article-title>
  • <fn-group>
  • <subtitle>
  • <trans-title-group>

May be contained in:

  • <article-meta>
  • <front-stub>
