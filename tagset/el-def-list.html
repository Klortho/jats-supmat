Element: def-list

Definition List
List in which each item consists of two parts: 1) a word, phrase, term, graphic, chemical structure, or equation, that is paired with 2) one or more descriptions, discussions, explanations, or definitions of it.

  • @list-content
  • @list-type
  • @prefix-word
  • @specific-use
  • @xml:lang
