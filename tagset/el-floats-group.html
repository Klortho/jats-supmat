Element: floats-group

Floating Element Group
Container element for floating objects (figures, tables, text boxes, graphics, etc.) that occur within an article. Occurs outside of the narrative flow of the article.

Content Model

( alternatives | boxed-text | chem-struct-wrap | fig | fig-group | graphic | media | preformat | supplementary-material | table-wrap | table-wrap-group )*

May Contain:

  • <alternatives>
  • <boxed-text>
  • <chem-struct-wrap>
  • <fig>
  • <fig-group>
  • <graphic>
  • <media>
  • <preformat>
  • <supplementary-material>
  • <table-wrap>
  • <table-wrap-group>

May be contained in:

  • <article>
  • <response>
  • <sub-article>
