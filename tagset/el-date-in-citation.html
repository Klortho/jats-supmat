Documentation for NISO-supmat-extension-rec0.dtd

Element: date-in-citation

Date within a Citation
Non-publication date used within a bibliographic reference. Replaces deprecated elements <access-date> and <time-stamp>.

Attributes

  • @calendar
  • @content-type
  • @iso-8601-date
  • @specific-use
  • @xml:lang

Content Model

( #PCDATA | day | month | season | year )*

May Contain:

  • PCDATA
  • <day>
  • <month>
  • <season>
  • <year>

May be contained in:

  • <element-citation>
  • <mixed-citation>
  • <product>
  • <related-article>
  • <related-object>
