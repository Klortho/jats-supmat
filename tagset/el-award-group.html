Element: award-group

Award Group
Container element for information concerning one award under which the work (or the research on which the work was based) was supported.

Attributes

  • @award-type
  • @id
  • @rid
  • @specific-use
  • @xlink:actuate
  • @xlink:href
  • @xlink:role
  • @xlink:show
  • @xlink:title
  • @xlink:type
  • @xml:lang
  • @xmlns:xlink

Content Model

( funding-source*, award-id*, principal-award-recipient*, principal-investigator* )

May Contain:

  • <award-id>
  • <funding-source>
  • <principal-award-recipient>
  • <principal-investigator>

May be contained in:

  • <funding-group>
